## Core terms

- **Tenant**: top-level organization boundary for data and permissions.
- **Delegated admin**: explicit cross-org permission grant under sponsor governance.
- **Shipment dossier**: assembled compliance packet before filing package generation.
- **Filing package**: versioned, pre-flight-gated submission payload.
- **Pre-flight**: mandatory blocker/warning + risk checks before submission.
- **Issue/exception**: tracked blocker or warning with owner and recovery path.
