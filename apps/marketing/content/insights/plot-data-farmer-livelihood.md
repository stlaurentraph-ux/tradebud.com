## Income follows access

Smallholders lose when compliance data lives in exporter silos. Farmers who own their GeoID can grant access to multiple buyers and cooperatives on their terms.

## One capture, many markets

Mapping a plot once through the mobile app creates a reusable passport. That reduces duplicate field visits and lets farmers participate in higher-value chains without repeating work.

## Free tier matters

Removing per-farmer fees is not charity — it is infrastructure. Livelihood gains scale when the base layer is free and open.
