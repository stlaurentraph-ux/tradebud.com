## Who can use a simplified declaration?

Micro and small primary operators in **low-risk countries** who produce commodities themselves may qualify for a one-time simplified declaration under EUDR. This reduces administrative burden while still preserving origin evidence.

## What you still need to capture

Even with a simplified path, authorities expect defensible origin data:

- Producer identity and contact details
- Plot geolocation (point or polygon depending on farm size)
- Commodity and production context
- Evidence that the plot was not subject to deforestation after the cut-off date

## Next steps

If you are unsure whether your operation qualifies, map your plots first and consult your buyer's due diligence requirements. Structured origin data is valuable whether you file simplified or full statements.
