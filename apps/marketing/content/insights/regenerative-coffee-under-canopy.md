## False positives hurt farmers

Rigid satellite algorithms often confuse seasonal pruning with deforestation. That penalises the very agroforestry systems buyers claim to want.

## Practice tied to place

Regenerative claims must link to specific plots and timestamps — identity preservation, not mass-balance pools.

## Photo vault as ground truth

Geo-tagged field photos give reviewers local context when automated checks flag amber. That keeps shade-grown coffee in market instead of in review limbo.
