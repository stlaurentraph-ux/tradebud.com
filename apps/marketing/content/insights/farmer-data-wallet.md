## The lock-in problem

Closed traceability platforms often trap farmer plot data inside a single buyer or exporter ecosystem. That makes it harder for farmers to switch markets or work with multiple partners.

## Request-grant instead of forced upload

Tracebud uses explicit grants. Farmers capture plots and evidence once, then approve which organizations can read or aggregate their GeoID for due diligence.

## Practical benefits

- Sell to more than one buyer without remapping the same farm
- Revoke access when a relationship ends
- Participate in cooperative roll-ups while retaining ownership of the underlying record
