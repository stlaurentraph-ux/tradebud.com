## What EUDR expects

Regulators and importers of record need defensible links between shipment weight and the geolocations that produced it. Blending origins into anonymous pools breaks that chain of custody.

## Identity-preserved batches

Each batch should trace to verified plot identifiers and farmer vouchers without laundering volume across unrelated origins.

## Yield-cap as a backstop

Even with identity preservation, exporters should cross-check delivery weight against the biological carrying capacity of contributing plots to flag illicit blending.
