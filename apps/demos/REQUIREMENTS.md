# Requirements for demo apps

This folder contains permanent public demos used for storytelling, validation, and onboarding.

## Canonical sources

- Strategic / commercial requirements: [REQUIREMENTS.md](../../REQUIREMENTS.md)
- Detailed product PRD: [PRODUCT_PRD.md](../../PRODUCT_PRD.md)
- App topology and deployment mapping: [apps/STRUCTURE.md](../STRUCTURE.md)

## Demo policy

- Demos should represent the canonical tier and role narratives.
- Demo behavior may be simplified, but core terminology and workflow intent must remain consistent.
- Product truth lives in `REQUIREMENTS.md` and `PRODUCT_PRD.md`; demos should not redefine requirements.
